About this Event

Herbert Grönemeyer, one of Germany's most successful and influential musicians, returns to the stage with his highly anticipated "Tumult Tour 2026". Following the massive success of his latest album, this tour promises an unforgettable evening filled with both classic hits and powerful new compositions.

Known for his energetic performances and deeply poetic lyrics, Grönemeyer continues to captivate audiences across generations. The Tumult Tour features a state-of-the-art stage production, bringing a modern visual dimension to his timeless sound. Don't miss the chance to experience one of Europe's greatest performers live in concert.
